Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a full detailing service take? +

It depends on the vehicle size and condition. Generally, a Type 1 vehicle takes 3–5 hours, while a Type 2 vehicle can take 5–8 hours for a complete interior and exterior restoration.

Do I need to book my appointment in advance? +

Yes, we operate strictly by appointment to ensure every vehicle receives our undivided attention and precision care. Please call us to secure your time slot.

What is the benefit of the Ceramic Spray Protection? +

Our ceramic spray adds a highly hydrophobic layer over your paint. It repels water, prevents dirt from bonding to the surface, and gives your car a deep, mirror-like gloss that lasts for weeks.

What if my car is extremely dirty? +

We take pride in tackling heavy challenges! Heavily soiled vehicles (excessive pet hair, deep mud, severe stains) require extra time and products, which is why they are individually assessed upon arrival.
